## 1. Project Overview

Jan Seva Saathi is an AI-powered citizen assistance platform designed to improve access to government services and schemes. The platform helps citizens understand their eligibility, required documents, application process, and reduces unnecessary visits to government offices by providing smart visit time estimation and token generation.

The system focuses on inclusion, accessibility, and real-world community impact, especially for rural and semi-urban populations.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Government offices often face long queues and overcrowding. Many citizens waste hours or even full days to access basic public services.

Major challenges faced by citizens:
- Lack of awareness about government schemes and services
- Confusion about required documents
- Difficulty understanding online vs offline processes
- Language barriers
- Low digital literacy
- No proper visit time management system in offices

---

## 3. Objectives

The main objectives of Jan Seva Saathi are:

- Provide easy access to government service information
- Help citizens check eligibility for schemes and services
- Provide document requirement guidance
- Reduce crowding in government offices
- Generate smart tokens with estimated visit time
- Support local languages and voice interaction
- Improve citizen-government interaction efficiency

---

## 4. Target Users

- Common citizens
- Rural and semi-urban population
- Senior citizens
- Low digital literacy users
- Users who prefer local language interaction
- People who prefer voice-based assistance

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

### 5.1 User Features
- User can access platform via mobile or web
- User can provide input via text or voice
- User can select preferred language

### 5.2 AI Assistance Features
- AI understands user intent (service or scheme requirement)
- AI checks eligibility based on user details
- AI provides step-by-step guidance
- AI suggests required documents

### 5.3 Service Guidance
- Show whether service is available online or offline
- Provide official portal links for online applications
- Provide office location details for offline services

### 5.4 Smart Token System
- Generate token number for office visit
- Provide estimated visit time slot
- Help reduce waiting time and crowding

### 5.5 Information Delivery
- Local language text explanation
- Voice-based response support

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- Fast response time
- High system availability
- Secure user data handling
- Scalable for large number of users
- Works in low bandwidth conditions
- Easy to use interface

---

## 7. Constraints

- Dependence on availability of government data APIs
- Internet connectivity limitations in rural areas
- Limited dataset availability for local languages
- Accuracy depends on data quality

## 8. Assumptions
